Use Case 00050:Browse portal participants

Release Target: 2.0

Description:

A Portal Participant wishes to browse through a listing of all participants in the portal.
Goal Level: user_goal
Actors:
  • Portal Participant
Trigger: A Portal Participant wishes to browse through a listing of all participants in the portal
Preconditions: A participants portlet must be installed in the Portal.
Basic Path:


  1. The Portal Participant navigates to the "Participants" listing screen for the portal.
  2. The participant is presented with a listing of all participants in the portal and certain frequently-needed information. If the number of participants exceeds some threshold, the list will be broken up into multiple pages, with links for quick access to partcipants whose names begin with particular letters of the alphabet.
  3. Each entry in the provided list will include a link to drill down to the "One Participant" view. See PSRV-00052 ("View a single participant's activities and roles within a portal") for a description of this view.
Postconditions:

Succesful End Conditions:

The user is able to view listings of participants within the portal, and drill down to detailed views of individual participants.
